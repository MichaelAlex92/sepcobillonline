How to Check Your SEPCO Bill Online

Step 1: Visit the SEPCO Website

  1. Open Your Browser: Launch your preferred web browser on your computer or mobile device.
  2. Go to SEPCO Bill Online Website: Enter the URL of SEPCO Bill Online website https://sepcobill.com.pk in the address bar and press Enter. This will take you to SEPCO Bill Online website.

Step 2: Navigate to the Input box

  1. Locate the Bill Inquiry Option: On the SEPCO homepage, look for the ‘Input box’ section where you'll enter your 14-digit Reference Number or 10-digit Customer ID.

Step 3: Enter Required Information

  1. Input Your Reference Number or Customer ID: enter your reference number or Customer ID. This number can usually be found on your previous bills

Step 4: View Your Bill

  1. Submit Your Information: After entering the required details, click on the "Check Bill" button.
  2. Check Your Bill Details: Your current bill details, including the total amount due and the due date, will be displayed on the screen.

Step 5: Download or Print Your Bill

  1. Print or Download the Bill: If you need a copy for your records, look for an option to print or download the bill in PDF format.
